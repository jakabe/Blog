Short and long term goals

Personally I think it's important to write out your own goals. Ideally they should be S.M.A.R.T.

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Attainable
  • Realistic
  • Time bound

Even if you can't achieve each of the SMART goal criteria, your goals should never be V.A.P.I.D.

  • Vague
  • Amorphous
  • Pie in the sky
  • Irrelevant
  • Delayed
